How to Play

With Doodl.io ("dew-dlee-oh"), four players will face off in three rounds of sketch-y charades. In each 60-second round, every player will get their chance to draw their provided prompt, and other players will attempt to guess the word or phrase before time runs out! Guessers get more points the faster they input the correct answer, and artists get points for correct guesses in a timely manner.
